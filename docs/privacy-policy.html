Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Short version: We collect only what the app needs to work. We never sell your data. You can delete everything at any time.

1. Who we are

FitAlarm is developed by rotiV3. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@rotiv3.com.

2. Data we collect and why

📍 Precise Location stored locally

  • Gym check-in: We use GPS to detect when you arrive at or leave a saved gym location.
  • Outdoor activity tracking: We record your GPS route during runs, walks, hikes and bike rides.
  • When collected: Only while you have an active tracking session or the gym check-in service is running.
  • Where stored: On your device only. Route data is never uploaded to our servers.

👤 Google Account Info stored locally

  • When you sign in with Google we receive your name, email address and profile photo.
  • We use this to personalise the greeting and to request Google Calendar access.
  • We do not store your Google credentials — authentication is handled entirely by Google.

📅 Calendar Events stored locally

  • With your permission (Pro feature) we read events from your Google or Apple Calendar.
  • Event titles, times, locations and descriptions are cached on your device to display in the app.
  • We do not modify, delete or share your calendar events.

🏆 Activity & Achievement Data stored locally

  • Gym sessions, outdoor sessions, planned activities and achievements are stored in a local database on your device.
  • This data is never uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly use a share feature.

3. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect health or biometric data (heart rate, steps, etc.)
  • We do not collect payment card details — billing is handled entirely by Google Play
  • We do not collect contacts, messages or photos
  • We do not run analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites

4. Data sharing

We never sell, rent or trade your personal data. never sold

The only third parties that may receive data are:

  • Google LLC — for Google Sign-In and Google Calendar API (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Apple Inc. — if you use Sign in with Apple (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Billing — processes Pro subscription payments (no card data touches our app)

5. Data retention

All activity data lives on your device. It is kept until you:

  • Delete individual records inside the app
  • Uninstall the app (clears all local data)
  • Submit a deletion request (see Section 6)

6. Your rights — data deletion

You have the right to request deletion of all data associated with your account at any time.

To delete your data:
• Inside the app: Settings → Account → Delete Account & Data
• Via web form: rotiv3.github.io/FitAlarm/data-deletion
• By email: privacy@rotiv3.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"

We will confirm deletion within 30 days. Because all data is stored locally, uninstalling the app immediately removes it from your device. deleted on request

7. Children's privacy

FitAlarm is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us immediately.

8. Security

Activity data is stored in an encrypted Room database on your device. We do not transmit personal data to our own servers, which means there is no server-side data breach risk for your activity history.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you via an in-app notice for material changes. The "Last updated" date at the top will always reflect the current version.

10. Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@rotiv3.com
