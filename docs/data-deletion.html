🗑️ Data Deletion

Request deletion of your FitAlarm account and all associated data

What gets deleted

  • Your Google / Apple account association with FitAlarm
  • All gym sessions and outdoor activity records
  • Planned activities you created in the app
  • Achievements and progress data
  • Saved gym locations
  • Pro subscription link (subscription itself managed via Google Play)
💡 Fastest way: Simply uninstall the app — all data stored on your device is immediately deleted. Use this form if you also want confirmation that no server-side records exist, or if you cannot access the app.

Option 1 — Delete directly in the app

1

Open FitAlarm

Make sure you are signed in to the account you want to delete

2

Go to Settings

Tap the ⚙️ icon on the Home screen

3

Account → Delete Account & Data

Confirm the deletion — all local data is removed immediately

Option 2 — Submit a request here

✅ Request received

We will process your deletion request within 30 days and send a confirmation to your email address.

📧 You can also email us directly at privacy@rotiv3.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and your account email. We respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.
